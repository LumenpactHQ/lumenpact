# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in Lumenpact, please report it privately and do not open a public issue.

Please email the maintainers at security@lumenpact.dev with:

- a description of the vulnerability,
- the affected component,
- steps to reproduce,
- and any suggested mitigation.

## Disclosure expectations

We ask reporters to allow a reasonable amount of time for a coordinated response before public disclosure. We will investigate promptly and keep you informed of the status of the fix.

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- Soroban smart contract logic,
- frontend integration code,
- deployment configuration,
- and wallet-related flows that could affect funds or user trust.

## Safe harbor

We support responsible disclosure and will not pursue legal action against researchers who report vulnerabilities in good faith and follow this process.
