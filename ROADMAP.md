# Roadmap

Lumenpact is currently focused on making the core escrow workflow reliable, understandable, and contributor-friendly.

## Near term

- stabilize the contract lifecycle on Stellar testnet,
- improve documentation and onboarding for new contributors,
- strengthen automated testing around contract and frontend flows,
- refine the judge experience in the web app.

## Medium term

- add richer evidence submission flows,
- expand contract and UI tests,
- improve deployment automation and release hygiene,
- and make the architecture easier to reason about for external contributors.

## Long term

- support stronger proof mechanisms or oracle-assisted verification,
- explore mobile experiences,
- and broaden integrations with the Stellar ecosystem.
